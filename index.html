Omar Alhussein

I am a senior research engineer with the advanced networking team at Huawei Technologies Canada, Ottawa. I also serve on the editorial board of Springer's Peer-to-Peer Networking and Applications Journal. Currently, my focus is on developing native machine learning based ideas and solutions for next-generation (6G) networks.

I received my Ph.D. degree with the BBCR lab at the University of Waterloo under the supervision of Prof. Weihua Zhuang. My thesis focused on the orchestration and provisioning of network function virtualization enabled multicast services.

My MASc was obtained from Simon Fraser university under the supervision of Prof. Jie Liang and Prof. Sami Muhaidat. My thesis was on the performance analysis of generalized and composite wireless fading channels through the use of mixture distributions.

Please feel free to contact me for anything.

Research Interests

Networking and AI, mobile-edge & cloud computing, SDN/NFV, wireless communications.
